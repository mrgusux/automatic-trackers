## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- 🔀 Reviewing and merging Pull Requests
- 🏗️ Protecting architectural integrity (`scripts/update.sh` as the single source of truth)
- 🧹 Keeping the sanitization pipeline and `blacklist.txt` exclusion filtering intact
- 🌐 Curating `config/sources.txt` and `config/blacklist_sources.txt` (adding healthy sources, pruning dead ones)
- ⚙️ Keeping GitHub Actions workflows secure, fast, and green
- 🚨 Triaging security reports per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)

## Becoming a Maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributors may be invited to become maintainers. The path:

1. Make several merged contributions (code, sources, docs, or reviews) — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
2. Demonstrate sound judgment in issues, reviews, and discussions
3. Be nominated by an existing maintainer per [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md)
